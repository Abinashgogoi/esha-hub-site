Hub FAQs

What does ESHA Hub do?

ESHA Hub empowers innovators by connecting bright minds with the tools, mentorship, and opportunities they need to turn ideas into reality — creating a collaborative ecosystem for research, innovation, and growth.

What fields do you support?

We support ethical and future-driven advancements across fields like artificial intelligence, health technology, space research, sustainable systems, and other emerging domains — all aimed at building a smarter, safer, and better tomorrow.

Who is on the team?

Our team brings together young researchers, engineers, and creators driven by a shared vision — to push boundaries, explore new possibilities, and build innovations that create real-world impact.

Who are your supporters?

We collaborate with organizations such as Atmanirbhar Assam (CMAAA 2.0) and other development programs to expand our reach and empower innovative, research-driven initiatives.

What are your milestones?

From successful projects to impactful government collaborations, ESHA Hub takes pride in its early achievements and remains dedicated to continuous innovation and growth.
